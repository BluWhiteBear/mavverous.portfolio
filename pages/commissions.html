About My Commission Services

I create custom digital artwork for individuals and businesses across a variety of styles and purposes. Whether you need concept art for a game character, a company logo, or a custom illustration, I work closely with clients to bring their vision to life with attention to detail and professional quality.

The Commission Process

1. Submit Request

Fill out my commission request form on Google Forms with details about your project, including type of artwork, dimensions, purpose, references, and any specific requirements.

2. Consultation & Quote

I'll review your request and respond within 48 hours with a quote based on the complexity, size, and time required. We'll discuss any questions and finalize the project details.

3. Payment

Once we agree on the details, a 50% deposit is required to begin work. The remaining balance is due upon completion before final files are delivered.

4. Initial Sketches

I'll create preliminary sketches or concepts for your approval before proceeding to the final artwork. This is your opportunity to request minor adjustments.

5. Final Artwork

After sketch approval, I'll create the final artwork. You'll receive progress updates, and you can request up to two rounds of minor revisions at this stage.

6. Delivery

Upon final approval and receipt of remaining payment, I'll deliver the high-resolution files in your preferred format (PNG, JPG, PDF, etc.).

Frequently Asked Questions

Timeframes vary depending on the complexity of the project and my current workload. Simple logos or character busts typically take 5-7 business days, while detailed illustrations or character sheets may take 2-3 weeks. I'll provide a specific estimate for your project during our consultation.

My standard commission includes one round of major revisions at the sketch phase and two rounds of minor revisions during the final artwork phase. Major changes after the sketch phase has been approved may incur additional fees, which I'll discuss with you before proceeding.

For digital illustrations and character art, I typically provide high-resolution JPG and PNG files. For logos and branding work, I provide vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) along with PNG and JPG formats. If you need a specific file format, please mention it in your request.

Client satisfaction is my priority. If you're not happy with the final artwork, I'll work with you to address your concerns within the scope of the original commission. That's why I provide progress updates and approval stages throughout the process to ensure we're on the same page.

The 50% deposit is non-refundable once I've begun work on your commission. If you need to cancel before work begins, I can provide a full refund. If you cancel after the sketch phase but before final artwork, 50% of your deposit may be refundable depending on the circumstances.
