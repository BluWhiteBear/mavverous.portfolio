Cusdis Comments Setup Guide

Step 1: Create a Cusdis Account

  1. Go to https://cusdis.com
  2. Sign up for a free account using GitHub, Google, or email

Step 2: Create a New Website

  1. After logging in, click on "New Website" button
  2. Enter your website title (e.g., "Mavverous Portfolio")
  3. Enter your website URL (e.g., "https://mavverous.com")
  4. Click "Create"

Step 3: Get Your App ID

  1. From your dashboard, select the website you just created
  2. Look for your App ID in the dashboard
  3. Copy this ID - you'll need it for the next step

Step 4: Update Your Artwork Detail Page

  1. Open pages/artwork-detail.html in your code editor
  2. Find the Cusdis container code:
    3. <div id="cusdis_thread" data-host="https://cusdis.com" data-app-id="YOUR_APP_ID" data-page-id="" data-page-url="" data-page-title=""> </div>
  3. Replace YOUR_APP_ID with the App ID you copied in Step 3

Step 5: Test Your Comments

  1. Open your website and navigate to an artwork detail page
  2. Scroll down to the comments section
  3. Test by adding a comment - it should appear after approval in your Cusdis dashboard

Step 6: Managing Comments

  1. Log into your Cusdis dashboard at https://cusdis.com/dashboard
  2. From here, you can:
    • Approve or reject comments
    • Delete comments
    • Reply to comments
    • Change moderation settings
